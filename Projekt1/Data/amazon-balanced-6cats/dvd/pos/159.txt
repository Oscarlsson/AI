Synopsis
Swedish indie pop combo the Cardigans deliver a stellar performance for their British fans in this performance video. The Cardigans: Live in London was recorded during a concert at the Shepherds Bush Empire in late 1996, as the group were touring in support of their breakthrough album, First Band on the Moon. The 16-song set includes "Lovefool," "Rise and Shine," "Your New Cuckoo," "Nasty Sunny Beam," "Never Recover," and more
