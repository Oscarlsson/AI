In August 1969 five session singers were invited to form a new session group. Main singer was Tony Burrows of Ivy League and Flowerpot Men fame. The first success came with the second single United We Stand. This CD  collects together the three US hits of the session group plus the only US  hit of the group that was the face of the session group in concert during  1971-1973 and that would become the second outfit of the group, the  Eurovision winning song Save Your Kisses For Me.
